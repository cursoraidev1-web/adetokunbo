## Review Collection Tips

### How to Get More Reviews:

1. **After Successful Outcome**
   - Email clients after job offer / admission
   - Ask about their experience
   - Request brief testimonial

2. **What to Ask**
   - "What was your main challenge before working with us?"
   - "What result did you achieve?"
   - "How did our service help you?"
   - "What would you tell others considering our service?"

3. **Keep It Simple**
   - 1-3 sentences maximum
   - Focus on specific results
   - Natural language (not overly polished)
   - Permission to use initials

4. **Incentivize Feedback**
   - Offer discount on future services
   - Thank you note or gift
   - Feature them as success story (with permission)
